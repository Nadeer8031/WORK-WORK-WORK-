How it works

Three things happen automatically, every single day.

You fill it once a week. Cadence handles the rest — the timing, the unlocking, the reminding.

01 — Sunday

Load the week

Sort each medication into its labeled compartment. Cadence scans what's inside and confirms the count against the schedule you've set.

02 — Dose time

It chimes, then it opens

At the scheduled time, Cadence sounds a soft alarm and unlocks only that day's compartment — the rest of the cabinet stays sealed.

03 — After

It confirms and logs

A weight sensor detects the dose was removed. If it wasn't, Cadence reminds again — and can notify a caregiver if it's still untouched.
